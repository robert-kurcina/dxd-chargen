
# Prompt Idea: Character Attributes

You are a game master explaining the primary character attributes in the Sarna Len RPG to a new player. Describe what each attribute represents and how it is used in the game.

**Core Concept:**

In Sarna Len, your character's fundamental strengths and weaknesses are defined by a set of 12 core **Attributes**. These scores typically range from 4 to 14 for a starting character, with 7 being average. Each attribute has an **Interim Multiple (IM)**, which is a cost used for character creation and advancement. The attributes are divided into four thematic groups.

---

### Combat Attributes
These attributes govern your character's effectiveness in a fight.

*   **Close Combat Ability (CCA):** Governs your skill in melee combat, affecting your ability to hit opponents and avoid being hit. (IM: 5)
*   **Range Combat Ability (RCA):** Determines your accuracy with ranged weapons like bows and crossbows. (IM: 3)
*   **Reflexes (REF):** Represents your speed and reaction time. It's crucial for determining who acts first in combat (Initiative), detecting hidden things, dodging ranged attacks, and disengaging from melee. (IM: 9)

---

### Psychological Attributes
These attributes define your character's mental and social capabilities.

*   **Intellect (INT):** Your capacity for learning, reasoning, investigation, and problem-solving. (IM: 7)
*   **Knowledge (KNO):** Represents your memory, lore, and general education. It determines the maximum number of skills you can learn. (IM: 3)
*   **Presence (PRE):** Your charisma, empathy, and ability to influence others. It's used for negotiation, leadership, and social grace. (IM: 3)
*   **Willpower (POW):** Your mental fortitude. It's used to resist fear, overwhelming emotions, and to maintain discipline and morale. (IM: 4)

---

### Physical Attributes
These attributes relate to your character's physical prowess and constitution.

*   **Strength (STR):** Your raw physical power. It adds a bonus to damage in melee, and determines how much you can lift, carry, push, and throw. (IM: 5)
*   **Fortitude (FOR):** Your physical resilience. It's used to resist injury, poison, disease, and other physical afflictions. (IM: 6)
*   **Move (MOV):** Your agility and movement speed, determining how far you can travel in a combat turn. (IM: 8)
*   **Size (SIZ):** Represents your character's physical mass and bulk, indexed on the Universal Table. (IM: 5)

---

### Magic Attributes
This attribute is central to all magical and supernatural abilities.

*   **Zed (ZED):** Your connection to the flow of magic and probability. It governs your ability to use spells and can also be considered your "Luck" score. (IM: 5)
